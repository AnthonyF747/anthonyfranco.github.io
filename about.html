About Rat Terriers

Rat terriers are very flexible dogs meaning that they blend well with their owner.

Traits Reason
Loyal These dogs love their owners and blend well with their owner's personality
Activity Level Depending on their owner, rat terriers can be high-energy dogs
Training Since these dogs are intelligent, they require training but they are easily trainable
